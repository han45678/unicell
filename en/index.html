Company Profile

Long Term Care

Beauty Maintenance

Revitalize

Unicell Biotechnology is a distributor that imports primarily cell products from the U.S. that provides services and products for the nutrition, healthcare, and long-term care industries. Considering Taiwan’s aging population and the increased attention paid to healthcare among the general public, UNICELL Biotechnology (headquartered in Tainan) has established an exclusive cell-based ingredient distribution partnership with Invitrx Therapeutics in California.

Unicell has expressed its original intention to seek a healthy future. We provide comprehensive cell distribution services. Thanks to advancements in medical technology, our company has been exploring methods to the inevitable physical changes caused by aging and sickness. These have been recognized as pioneering cellular science – “regenerative medicine.” This novel medical method uses cell-based technology to repair or renew injured tissues, improving health condition caused by nature process

Unicell has applied cellular science in several areas, such as its recent development of cosmetic and healthcare products, which are generating more diverse possibilities for this technology.
